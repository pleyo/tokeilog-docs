TokeiLog: Simple Time Tracking

Track your work hours easily with a clean, privacy-focused time tracking app that keeps all your data on your device.

App Description

TokeiLog is a beautifully simple time tracking app designed for freelancers, consultants, and anyone who needs to log their work hours. With an intuitive interface and powerful features, TokeiLog helps you track your time without getting in your way.

Unlike other time tracking apps, TokeiLog is completely privacy-focused. There are no accounts to create, no data sent to the cloud, and no analytics tracking your usage. Your time entries stay on your device, giving you complete control over your data.
