Introduction

At TokeiLog, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our iOS app.

Data Collection

TokeiLog is designed to work completely offline, with all data stored locally on your device:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your usage of the app
  • We do not use analytics services
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Local Data Storage

All your time tracking data is stored solely on your device's local storage:

  • Time entries
  • Notes and labels
  • App preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the app's sharing features.

Permissions

TokeiLog does not request any special permissions from your device.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • Keeping your iOS updated to the latest version
  • Backing up your device regularly

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the app if necessary.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
